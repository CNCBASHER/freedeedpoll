How does this work?

You'll answer a few questions, then print a Deed Poll.

Then just sign your new Deed Poll and have it witnessed by somebody else: they'll need to sign it too. We suggest that you print it onto nice, heavy paper (ask a stationer for parchment paper), and that you make two or three copies at the same time.

Is this really free?

Yes. This is a completely free service.

As a British citizen, you have the right to be known by any name you choose. To show that you're serious about adopting your new name (and abandoning your old one), you can create a Deed Poll. This site helps you to do that, quickly and easily, and without any expensive lawyers.
